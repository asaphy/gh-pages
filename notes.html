Podcast Notes: Promotions and tooling at Google (The Pragmatic Engineer)

Asaph Yuan November 8, 2024
Design docs should contain:
  • Brief description of problem and goal
  • PRD link
  • High level architecture
  • Proposed interfaces
  • Risks
  • Timelines
  • Launch plan
Credibility = track record of results
  • Show understanding and listening well
  • Being reliable with executing
  • Be excited where you are
You can have your own career plan:

Work on those competencies
Work on projects to hit competencies
Focus on developing skills

Influence = input you give to your org to affect its output
  • Feedback
  • disagreement
  • sharing pov
  • Getting buy in
Why should people listen to you?
  • Who you are
  • How you present ideas
  • Should have key relationships
  • How can I be a better partner?
